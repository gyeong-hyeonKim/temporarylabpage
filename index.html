Our Mission

Our laboratory focuses on developing advanced artificial intelligence technologies to improve cancer diagnosis, treatment planning, and clinical decision support.

By integrating multimodal medical data such as MRI, pathology, genomic information, and clinical records, we aim to enable more accurate and personalized oncology care.

Impact

  • AI-powered precision oncology research
  • Clinical decision support systems
  • Multimodal medical data integration
